ABOUT

DENTAL WELLNESS is a purpose-designed holistic dentistry practice located at Johar Town Lahore. We offer dental services that ensure a safer, healthier, more beautiful smileWe support you as an individual and take into account the variety of factors that can lead to dental and oral health problems. Our approach to dental treatment is to focus on caring for a patient’s health and safety through an integration of both conventional and natural health philosophies.We pride ourselves in treating all our patients with openness and transparency and put you the patient in charge of your dental health, enabling us to achieve excellent results time after time. We believe passionately that creating health and beauty around us is an integral part of life and you will feel these qualities in our dental care.
